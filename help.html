Frequently Asked Questions

How do I take a test?

Navigate to the "Take Test" section from the dashboard menu. Select the desired test and click "Start Test." Follow the instructions to complete it.

How can I view my results?

Go to the "View Results" section in the dashboard menu. You'll see a table listing your previous tests, scores, and test dates. Click "View Details" for more information.

How do I reset my password?

Open the "Settings" section from the dashboard menu, enter a new password under "Change Password," and click "Save Changes." If unable to log in, use the "Forgot Password" option on the login page.
